I am an assistant professor at PMF, University of Zagreb, Croatia in the Theoretical Condensed Matter Physics division.

Until October 2022, I was a postdoc at the Institute for Theoretical Physics of University of Heidelberg in the group of Prof. Tilman Enss.

Previously, I was a postdoc at IST Austria in the group of Prof. Mikhail Lemeshko, supported by a Lise Meitner Fellowship of the FWF.

I got my Ph.D. at University of Padua with Prof. Luca Salasnich.

My main research focus is on analytical and computational approaches to many-body physics. My research interests include ultracold gases, quantum impurities, statistical mechanics, strongly-correlated electron systems and unconventional superconductivity.

For more information have a look at my publication list, look me up on arXiv or write me an email. You can also find my CV and some of my talks. Thanks for visiting!
